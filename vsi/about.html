OUR STORY

We are an industry trusted flooring contractor with over 25 years of experience. We specialize in flooring restoration, moisture vapor barrier installation, and concrete leveling. Our team is comprised of specialized, skillful, and masterful technicians when it comes to epoxy flooring, grinding, leveling, concrete polishing, and moisture barrier application. We also install carpet, sheet vinyl, tile, and wood solutions for commercial operations. We use eco grip flooring to really distinguish their level of professsionalism and knowledge in the flooring industry.

We have been completing projects for commercial clients in government, pharmaceutical, and healthcare markets. We have also been referred numerous times for flooring installations in the vastly expanding craft brewing scene.

We pride ourselves on being the best Epoxy Flooring Company in Southern California and take our reputation seriously. From the materials we use and endorse, to the rigorous certification process for installers, we give each and every customer the best floor possible. When it comes to deciding about your epoxy floors or decorative concrete needs, our slogan says it all, "The Wise Choice" when it comes down to flooring companies.
